What is search engine optimization ?

Search engine optimization (SEO) is the practice of improving the visibility and ranking of a website or web page in search engine results pages (SERPs), through effective use of search engine keywords. SEO can help increase traffic to a website from search engines, which can then lead to increased profits

Do I need SEO?

There is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, as the needs and requirements of a business will vary depending on its size, industry, and other factors. However, generally speaking, effective online marketing includes strategies such as search engine optimization (SEO), which is the process of improving the visibility of a website or web page in search engine results pages (SERPs). By positioning your website or web page higher in the SERPs, you can increase your chances of attracting more visitors and leads from potential customers. Additionally, SEO can help to improve the overall user experience by increasing the amount of traffic your website receives from search engines. If you are not currently using any form of online marketing and are interested in exploring options for increasing your website’s visibility and reach, SEO may be a good starting point.
